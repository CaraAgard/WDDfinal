The Wanderer

This Ghost has unfinished business in this world. It will wander around searching for answers as to why they are dead. It is not as dangerous as other ghosts. Do not make eye contact. It will see it as a threat and attack. You will often hear them crying in despair. If I'm feeling generous, I'll try to help the Wanderer find what they are looking for.
